Purpose of This App

This app was designed to make helping easier and to save lives as a community.To help neibhors be the first responders. Using this app, someone who is injured and needs help, can ask freinds/family or the neibhors for help. Also the patient or the first responders can alert nearest hospitals about the injuries and get directions to these hospitals. If the first responder can't take the patient to hospital, then they can alert other first responders and while waiting for someone, they can give simple treatments like CPR depending on the type of injury(using watson services).
